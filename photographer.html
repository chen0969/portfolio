COMMERICAL MAGICIAN

RELATIVE SKILLS:
Photography, Lighting, Composition, Color Theory, Commercial.

Little did they know, Jing-An's first job was as a commercial photographer and social media manager for a disposable tableware firm called JFY Retailer. Despite limited resources—a cheap smartphone and basic tools—he transformed ordinary tableware into captivating commercial photos, marketing materials, and social media posts. Jing-An's success was undeniable! Today, he effortlessly creates stunning photos for any subject and continues to serve JFY Retailer as their contracted photographer.

PHOTOS FOR SOCIETY

RELATIVE SKILLS:
Portrait, Post-Production, Adobe Software.

Today, Jing-An is a successful photographer, capturing commercial photos for various firms such as Capital E, a Taiwanese bag retailer. Additionally, he volunteers as a photographer at Algonquin College, creating numerous photos and albums for various events. Jing-An also manages the Instagram account Ottawa Free Photos, offering free photo services for tourists in Ottawa.

ANIMATED BY CINEMA

RELATIVE SKILLS:
Animation, Blender, Autodesk Software, SolidWorks, Story Board.

Jing-An's passion for cinema was ignited by his director aunt. He previously worked at an award-winning film firm in Taiwan called Green Film Production, where he contributed to various aspects of movie production, including artistic design, advertising, poster design, filming, and post-production. Jing-An harbors a personal dream of creating his own animated movie and has already produced numerous commercial animations for himself and various firms.
